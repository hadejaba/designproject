Meet Us

Take life by the slice.

Rey's is a local family-owned business started in 2021. Here at Rey's we welcome anyone and everyone regardless of age, sex, gender, sexual-orientation, or religion. Only requirement: you must love pizza!

Fresh life.

We have our slice bar, which we replenish every hour on the hour to maintain absolute freshness. Anything not in our slice bar is made TO ORDER! It may take longer but trust us, it is worth the wait. Pizza still left in the slice bar when replenishing is either given to staff or served to the homeless population in our community to ensure nothing goes to waste.

Eat Pizza. Drink Beer. Breathe Colorado.
